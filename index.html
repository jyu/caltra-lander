Caltra

Trello for humans, not companies

Caltra is a project management tool built to help you reach your dreams, whether it's hitting the gym or learning to dance. Break your goals down into cards, schedule them, and complete them with Caltra.

Features for your goals:

Card based work

Personal goals are complex problems. Caltra helps you break down your goals into cards consisting of manageable chunks of work.

Kanban style workflow

Drag your cards across different lists representing each stage of work. Feel the satisfaction of moving around cards as you work.

Goal analytics

All your cards are linked to one of your goals. Caltra shows your current progress for each goal and how much work was done.

Features for you:

Calendar integration

Caltra sync's with your calendar, helping you find time in your busy schedule to work towards your goals.

Wellness

Research shows reflecting on your work helps you feel more satisfied. Caltra also provides motivation to make work easier.

Connect with experts

Talk to real humans about figuring out what your goals are or coming up with a strategy to tackle then.
